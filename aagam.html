Aagam

Aagam refers to Jain scriptures containing teachings of Tirthankaras. These sacred texts guide spiritual growth, moral conduct, ahimsa (non-violence), and understanding of reality.

📚 What are Aagams?

Jain Aagams are ancient scriptures preserved through oral tradition. They are divided into:

  • 12 Angas
  • Upangas
  • Cheda Sutras
  • Mula Sutras

They teach: Ahimsa, Aparigraha, Truth, Discipline, and Moksha path.
